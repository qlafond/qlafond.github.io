Course Description

Single variable integration and the fundamental theorem of Calculus. The emphasis is on the computation of areas, volumes, and integration of elementary (i.e., polynomial, rational, trigonometric, exponential, and logarithmic) functions. Advanced integration techniques, and improper integrals are discussed. Applications of calculus to physical situations, and probability are discussed
